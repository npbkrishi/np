About Us

We started this farm in 2068 B.S. with a vision to provide quality agricultural products to our community. Over the years, we have cultivated a variety of crops, including list of products like Cauliflower, Cabbage, Tomatoes, Chilies, etc. None of this would have been possible without the support of our valued customers and supporters. Thank you for being a part of our journey!

The N.B Krishi Farm is located in Malarani-5 Bangi Baghari, Arghakganchi, Nepal. Established in the year 2068, the N.B Krishi Farm is a family-driven endeavor. At Krishi Info, we're dedicated to providing you with the latest and most valuable information in the world of agriculture and farming. Whether you're a seasoned farmer or just getting started, our platform is here to empower you with knowledge, tips, and resources to make your agricultural journey a success.

Explore our wide range of articles, guides, and expert advice covering topics such as crop cultivation, livestock management, sustainable farming practices, market trends, and much more. Our mission is to support farmers in making informed decisions that lead to higher yields, healthier crops, and thriving farms.
